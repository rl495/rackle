About Me!


Hi! I'm Rachel, studying Chemical Engineering at Cornell University!

When I was young, I thought I would be following in my parents' footsteps and entering the medical field to become a doctor. However, I failed to find a passion in this field and decided to, instead, pursue engineering. After scoring significantly below the ~mean~ on my first Intro to ChemE prelim, I immediately wanted to switch majors. Mainly due not knowing what to switch into, I decided to stick it out. Thankfully, I began to appreciate and take advantage of Cornell's community and resources. After (somehow) joining two project teams (after being rejected from 7!), I was able to find a passion and orient myself in the right direction. Thanks to my indecisiveness and confusion, I failed to switch out of ChemE, which brings us to today.

I am a passionate, motivated student, currently most interested in the applications of engineering with medicine, food science, and consumer goods. I would like to be doing anything to make someone's daily life a little easier. Please check out my skills and hobbies pages to learn a little bit more about me! (also highly recommend random page for a fun time).







Thanks to my inspirational CS friends, my online professor, and a bit of motivation, I was able to complete this amazing project! I am grateful to dump the most random things here and learn so much on the way!
